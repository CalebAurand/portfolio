Home Grade Book

full-stack dev

I built Home Grade Book as a free and simple resources that small schools, or home-schooling parents can use to easily keep track of their class assignments, and student's grades.

To try out the app for yourself, feel free to send me an email at caurand13@gmail.com and I can set you up with an example teacher login and password so you can see some example classes and students that have already been added.

To see and use the app you can go to https://home-grade-book.vercel.app/login
